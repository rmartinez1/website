About The Photographers

Josue Lopez

Josue Lopez born and raised in Baldwin, New York

Josue Lopez interest in photography started when he was 16.

He took a class in highschool, Photography 1, which would spark a passion for photography.

After high school he decided to major in Media Communication.

He would go on to take photography classes to help improve his skills as a photographer

Shortly after starting college he landed a job as an assistant for a sport photographer.

He quickly learned how to edit pictures with the help of the sport photographer.

After a year Josue decided to part ways with the photographer and start his own business in photography.

Josue Lopez is currently a student at Molloy College majoring in psychology and continues to photograph.

David Lopez

David Lopez born and raised in Baldwin, New York.

David Lopez gainied an interest in photography after he began vlogging for his Youtube channel.

David Lopez would join his brother Josue in filming and photography for weddings.

His transition from filming to photography was smooth with the help of his brother.

David continues to vlog and help his brother in his photography business
